---
title: "4. How to check the Confirmed Allocations in Random Allocation?"
published: true
---

**a.** In the left-hand menu, go to the **Activities** tab. Click the **“View Activities”** button for an Activity Container with **Random Allocation** type.

![Activities → View Activities](/help/4-4-1.jpg)

**b.** Then go to the **Allocations Overview** step and click the **“Confirmed Allocations”** tab.

![Allocations Overview → Confirmed Allocations](/help/4-4-2.jpg)

**c.** Here you can see the list of **Confirmed Allocations**.

![Confirmed Allocations list](/help/4-4-3.jpg)

**d.** Open the dropdown of a **Student** to view and update the **Activities** for that student.

![Open student dropdown → view/update activities](/help/4-4-4.jpg)
