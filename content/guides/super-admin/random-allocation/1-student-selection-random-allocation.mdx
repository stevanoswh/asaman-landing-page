---
title: "1. How to do Student Selection in Random Allocation in ASAMAN?"
published: true
updatedAt: "2025-01-15"
---

**a.** In the left-hand menu, go to the **Activities** tab. Click the **“View Activities”** button for an Activity Container with a **Random Allocation** type.

![](/hhelp/4-1-1.jpg)

**b.** Then click on the **“Student Selection”** step. A student list will appear in a table below.

![](/help/4-1-2.jpg)

**c.** Click on a student’s name, and a **“Student Preference”** sidebar will appear on the right, where you can view their Activities for the **preferred activity day**.

![](/help/4-1-3.jpg)

**d.** Click on the **“Manage Activities”** button on the right sidebar. Here, you can give **Preference** to an Activity for each day for the student.

![](/help/4-1-4.jpg)

In this way, you can perform **Student Selections** for specific Activities on a day, for the whole week.
