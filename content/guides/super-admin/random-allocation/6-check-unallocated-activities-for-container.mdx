---
title: "6. How do you check Unallocated Activities for an Activity Container in Random Allocation?"
published: true
---

**a.** In the left-hand menu, go to the **Activities** tab. Click the **“View Activities”** button for an Activity Container with **Random Allocation** type.

![Activities → View Activities](/help/4-6-1.jpg)

**b.** Go to the **Allocations Overview** step and click the **“Unallocated”** tab.

![Allocations Overview → Unallocated](/help/4-6-2.jpg)

**c.** A list of **Students who are not allocated** to any of their preferred Activities will appear.  
Click on a Student to view their **unallocated Activities**.

![Unallocated students list](/help/4-6-3.jpg)
