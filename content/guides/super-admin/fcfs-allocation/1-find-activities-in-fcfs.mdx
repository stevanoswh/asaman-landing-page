---
title: "1. How to find Activities in First Come First Serve (FCFS) Allocation?"
published: true
---

**a.** In the left-hand menu, go to the **Activities** tab. Click the **“View Activities”** button for an Activity Container with an **FCFS** allocation type.  
Under the **Activities** step, you can find all the Activities in that Activity Container.

![Activities → FCFS → List of Activities](/help/5-1-1.jpg)

**b.** Click the **“View Details”** button for an Activity to see more information.

![Activity → View Details](/help/5-1-2.jpg)
