---
title: "3. How can I view Activities in a Container?"
published: true
---

In the left-hand menu, go to the **Activities** tab.  
Click the **“View Activities”** option for any Activity Container.

![Open Activities and choose “View Activities”](/help/3-3-1.jpg)

All the Activities in that container will appear.  
You can view the details of each Activity here.

![List of activities inside the container, click “View Details”](/help/3-3-2.jpg)
