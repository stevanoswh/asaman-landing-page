---
title: "1. How to add users to my account and view their details?"
published: true
---

After logging in to the **ASAMAN Super Admin** account, go to the **User Management** from the left-hand menu.  
From here, you can **add new users** and **view other details** for the users, such as **User Types** and **Action** buttons under the three dots in each row.
