---
title: "6. How can I send an Activation Email to a Parent user?"
published: true
---

**a.** In the left-hand menu, go to the **User Management** tab, and click on the name of any **Parent** user with **“Pending”** status.

![Select a Parent with Pending status](/help/2-6-1.jpg)

**b.** The Parent user profile will open. Now, click the **“Send Activation Email”** button in the top-right corner.

![Send Activation Email button on Parent profile](/help/2-6-2.jpg)

**c.** A modal will appear where you can add a custom message for the Parent user. Then click the **“Send Email”** button to send an activation email to the user.

![Activation email modal](/help/2-6-3.jpg)
