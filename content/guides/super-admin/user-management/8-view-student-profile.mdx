---
title: "8. How can I view the profile of a Student user?"
published: true
sort: 80
updatedAt: "2025-01-15"
---

**a.** In the left-hand menu, go to the **User Management** tab, and click on the name of any **Student** user to open
their profile. Here, you can view the basic details of the Student user on the left side and others in the
**Overview** tab.

![Student overview](/help/2-8-1.jpg)

<br />

**b.** In the **Diary** tab, you can view the **Activities** details for a Student in **Daily**, **Weekly**, & **Monthly** views.

![Student diary](/help/2-8-2.jpg)

<br />

**c.** In the **Attendance** tab, you can view the **Attendance** details for a Student in their enrolled **Activities**.

![Student attendance](/help/2-8-3.jpg)

<br />

**d.** In the **Reports** tab, you can view the **Activity reports** for a Student.

![Student reports](/help/2-8-4.jpg)
