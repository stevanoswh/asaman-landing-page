---
title: "10. How can I view the profile of a Staff user?"
published: true
---

**a.** In the left-hand menu, go to the **User Management** tab, and click on the name of any **Staff** user to open their profile.  
Here, you can view the basic details of the Staff user on the left side and others in the **Overview** tab.

![Staff Overview](/help/2-10-1.jpg)

---

**b.** In the **Schedule** tab, you can view all the scheduled, ongoing, and upcoming **Activities** in **Daily, Weekly, and Monthly** views.

![Staff Schedule](/help/2-10-2.jpg)

---

**c.** In the **Attendance** tab, you can view the attendance details for that **Staff** in their assigned **Activities**.

![Staff Attendance](/help/2-10-3.jpg)

---

**d.** In the **Reports** tab, you can view the **Activity reports** for **Students**.

![Staff Reports](/help/2-10-4.jpg)

---

**e.** In the **Chats** tab, you can find all kinds of communication that has happened with that **Staff** user.

![Staff Chats](/help/2-10-5.jpg)

---

**f.** You can access and update the **Settings** of the Staff user profile from the **Gear** icon at the top.

![Open Staff Settings](/help/2-10-6.jpg)

---

**g.** A modal will appear where you can view and update details for the Staff user.

![Staff Settings Modal](/help/2-10-7.jpg)
